Identifying Pain Points

"Customers want to gain the price with at least money & time spend as possible, to make this happened suitable amount of support and help offer timing is a key to that.  "
1 - Depression

Customers are depressed by the helpless feeling when fail to catch the price. Some of them will gain a bad impression like this company would let the player win the price, and result in the loss of customers.

2 - Not having enough support from storekeepers

Storekeepers heavy, multiple responsible, from manage rainbow pools, accounting, other entrainment machine caretaker, add goods, cleaning, etc. It’s difficult for customers to seek help when needs.

3 - Bad practice with the payment

Membership IC card is not only inconvenient but also increase the workload of storekeepers.  
`Moreover, in most cases, customers get anxious when they out of money in the card. Customers used to rush for value-adding while worries others will play the machine.

Based on findings...The design should aims

A happy customer equals a more successful business. To balance between business’s decisions and customers’ satisfaction, instead of optimize the difficulty of claw, we could provide more support to help them gain the price.

To attract customer, no only discount tickets could be provided. Credit points could also be promoted for the customers.

E-payment method
