EduHK Programme Website

UI Design
Web Design
Programming
When I am still working in EduHK, I was ask in assist and create a webpage for a new minor programme for promotion matters.
I was responsible mainly in programme while partly design for the website.

Major issues duel with

The major problem during the whole website building process was we have to consist with the art style of department's homepage.
‍
As the target students for the programme are young in age. The website was aims to have a better and refreshing design for promotion matters.
‍
Under this situation, there's have a great limit with the design. After discussion with different colleagues, the website was created which strike a balance between.

Color & typography

The color and typography used was according to the logo of the Eduhk and department's color.
Homepage of the department.

Final design

Fast & clear layout for data navigation

The programme leader of this had a requirement which is makes everything fast and easy for finding the details user wants.
‍
Therefore, after discussion the website which designed to display information with tags. All the information are summarised and in well orders.
‍
You may visit the following link for the details as well: https://www.eduhk.hk/ci/ctl_minor/ci_ctlminor.html

Findings

Communication with ‘customers'

The whole project was for internal usage, however there are many limits and requirements for the whole design. Communications is the most important skills through out the whole progress. To discussion and understand the needs and through from the customers so you could full-fill their needs and make suggests for better design / performance.
